## üìò FRAMEWORK STRUCTURE

### Section 1: Compliance Principles
- **8 Core Principles**: Zero tolerance for fraud, mandatory identity, transparent operations, safety first, no intermediaries, privacy protection, documentation hygiene, continuous compliance

### Section 2: Compliance Domains
- **6 Primary Domains**: User Identity, Vehicle, Operational, Safety, Franchise, Admin compliance

### Section 3: Compliance Lifecycle
- **3 Phases**: Pre-Entry, Live Operation, Post-Operation compliance

### Section 4: Auto-Block Rules (Mandatory)
- **20+ Auto-Block Triggers**: Truck, Driver, Operator, Franchise, Admin violation triggers

### Section 5: Escalation System
- **4 Escalation Tiers**: Operational, Compliance Breach, Fraud Suspicion, Confirmed Fraud
- **Escalation Path**: Unit ‚Üí District ‚Üí HQ ‚Üí Admin ‚Üí MD

### Section 6: Risk Scoring System
- **4 Entity Types**: Operators, Drivers, Trucks, Franchises
- **Risk Score Components**: Multiple factors per entity type
- **Score Impact**: Assignment preference, incentives, investigations

### Section 7: Franchise Compliance Responsibilities
- **Unit Franchise**: Accurate inspections, timely verification, geo-tagging, rejection of non-compliant trucks
- **District Franchise**: Audit unit work, quality scorecard, escalations, compliance records
- **HQ**: Policy enforcement, surprise audits, franchise incentives, fraud database

### Section 8: Admin Compliance Boundaries
- **Authorized Actions**: Override shipments, reassign trucks/drivers, handle disputes, freeze shipments, investigate fraud
- **Prohibited Actions**: Access KYC without authorization, edit ledger, downgrade severity, remove auto-block, approve ineligible entities

### Section 9: Compliance Audit System
- **4 Audit Frequencies**: Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly
- **Audit Scope**: Tracking, shipments, compliance flags, franchise performance, risk analysis, fraud patterns

### Section 10: Consequences
- **4 Consequence Levels**: Low (warnings), Medium (temporary blocks), High (suspensions), Critical (permanent bans)

### Section 11: Compliance Framework Enforcement
- **Enforcement Methods**: System-level, business logic validation, franchise oversight, admin oversight
