## üìò LAWBOOK STRUCTURE

### Section 1: Definitions (Legally Binding)
- 15 core definitions (Booking, Bid, Shipment, Operator, Driver, Franchise, Admin, MD, KYC, POD, Ledger, Auto-Block, Strike, GPS Tracking, Inspection)

### Section 2: Zero-Compromise Rules
- **18 absolute business rules** that cannot be changed except by Managing Director
- Rules cover: Truck Eligibility, Driver Identity, OTP Delivery, Ledger Integrity, Bidding, Price Visibility, Tracking, Communication, Payments, KYC Access, Auto-Block, Franchise Authority, Fraud Tolerance, Truck Limits, Shipment Limits, Cancellation, Auto-Finalization, Alternate Trucks

### Section 3: Violation Categories & Penalty Matrix
- **Category A**: Minor Violations (warnings, training)
- **Category B**: Operational Violations (penalty scores, temporary blocks)
- **Category C**: Compliance Violations (auto-blocks, re-verification)
- **Category D**: Major Violations (immediate blocks, investigations)
- **Category E**: Critical Violations (permanent bans, legal escalation)

### Section 4: Penalty Enforcement Authority
- **Unit Franchise**: Category A + B (initial)
- **District Franchise**: Category B + C (full)
- **HQ (Admin)**: Category D + E (full)
- **Managing Director**: Ultimate authority

### Section 5: Appeals Process
- 4-stage appeals process (User ‚Üí District ‚Üí HQ ‚Üí MD)
- Appeal eligibility and rejection conditions
- Appeal outcomes (upheld, modified, reversed)

### Section 6: Franchise Liability Rules
- Liability scope (7 violation types)
- Strike system (1-4 strikes with escalating consequences)
- Franchise penalties (payout suspension, legal action, termination)
- Termination conditions (6 trigger conditions)

### Section 7: Admin Misconduct Rules
- 10 prohibited admin actions
- Violation penalties (deactivation, audit, termination)
- Override requirements (HQ authorization, documentation)
- Audit requirements (real-time logging, weekly/monthly reviews)

### Section 8: Zero Tolerance Fraud Policy
- Fraud definition (10 fraud types)
- Zero tolerance enforcement (permanent blocks, legal escalation)
- Fraud detection protocol (5 detection methods)
- Fraud prevention measures (7 prevention mechanisms)

### Section 9: Legal Escalation Triggers
- 8 mandatory escalation conditions
- 4-stage escalation process
- Escalation authority (HQ/MD only)
- Documentation requirements (7-year retention)

### Section 10: Final Clause
- Lawbook authority (overrides all other documents)
- Lawbook scope (all users, all products, all systems)
- Modification authority (MD only)
- Enforcement responsibility (system, franchise, HQ, MD levels)
- Acknowledgment requirement (mandatory for platform access)
