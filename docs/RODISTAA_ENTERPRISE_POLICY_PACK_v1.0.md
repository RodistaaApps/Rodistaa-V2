## üüß SECTION 3 ‚Äî USER IDENTITY & VERIFICATION POLICY

### 3.1 KYC Mandatory

**Policy**: Shipper, Operator, Driver must complete KYC before business operations.

**Enforcement**:
- Platform access blocked until KYC approved
- No booking creation without KYC
- No bidding without KYC
- No shipment assignment without KYC

**KYC Requirements**:
- Identity proof (Aadhaar, PAN, Driving License, or Passport)
- Address proof (Aadhaar, Utility Bill, or Bank Statement)
- Recent photograph matching identity document

---

### 3.2 Identity Verification Standards

**Verification Requirements**:

#### Government-Issued ID
- Valid, non-expired government-issued identity document
- Clear, readable document images
- Document authenticity verification

#### Driver License Verification
- Valid driving license (for drivers)
- Appropriate license class (HGV for truck drivers)
- License expiry tracking and validation

#### Face Match Validation
- Facial recognition technology
- Photo-to-document comparison
- Video verification if required
- In-person verification during inspection (for drivers)

#### Geo-Tagged Photos
- Drivers: Geo-tagged photo during inspection
- Trucks: Geo-tagged photo during inspection
- Location verification for authenticity

---

### 3.3 KYC Access Control

**KYC Access Restrictions**:

#### KYC-Admin Only
- **Only KYC-admin may view full documents**
- Complete, unmasked KYC data access
- Identity verification authority

#### All Other Roles
- **All other roles see masked KYC**
- Partial/masked information only
- Privacy protection enforced

**Violation**: Unauthorized KYC access triggers immediate investigation and potential termination.

---

### 3.4 Identity Rejection

**KYC is Rejected if**:

#### Mismatch
- Photo does not match identity document
- Information mismatch between documents
- Identity verification failure

#### Forgery
- Fake or forged identity documents
- Document authenticity check failed
- Suspicious document patterns

#### Alteration
- Digitally altered documents
- Tampered information
- Manipulated images

#### Tampering
- Physical tampering detected
- Document integrity compromised
- Evidence of modification

#### Stolen Identity
- Identity theft confirmed
- Duplicate identity detected
- Stolen document usage

**Rejection Process**:
1. System flags suspicious KYC
2. KYC-admin reviews and rejects
3. User notified of rejection
4. Account blocked until valid KYC submitted

---

### 3.5 Identity Lock

**Policy**: Immediately lock account upon fraud suspicion.

**Lock Triggers**:
- Suspicious KYC submission
- Identity mismatch detected
- Document forgery suspected
- Identity theft suspected
- Pattern of violations

**Lock Process**:
1. Account automatically locked
2. Investigation initiated
3. KYC re-verification required
4. Account unlocked only after verification complete

**Legal Escalation**: Confirmed identity theft triggers legal action and permanent ban.

## üü® SECTION 4 ‚Äî TRUCK COMPLIANCE & SAFETY POLICY

### 4.1 Mandatory Eligibility

**All trucks must meet ALL eligibility criteria**:

#### HGV Only
- Heavy Goods Vehicle (HGV) classification
- Open body or container trucks
- No light commercial vehicles

#### 2018+ Model
- Manufacturing year 2018 or later
- Model year verification required
- Pre-2018 trucks not eligible

#### BS4 or BS6
- Bharat Stage 4 (BS4) or Bharat Stage 6 (BS6) emission standard
- Emission standard verification
- Pollution Certificate (PUC) required

#### National Permit Mandatory
- Valid National Permit required
- Permit expiry tracking
- Auto-block if permit expired

**No Exceptions**: All criteria must be met. No exemptions without Managing Director approval.

---

### 4.2 Mandatory Inspections

**Inspection Requirements**:

#### Every 120 Days
- Mandatory physical inspection every 120 days
- Inspection cycle from last inspection date
- No extensions or exemptions

#### Unit Franchise Performs Inspection
- Unit Franchise conducts physical inspection
- Complete inspection checklist
- Photo documentation with geotag and timestamp

#### District Franchise Performs Audit Review
- District Franchise audits Unit Franchise inspections
- Quality control and accuracy verification
- Random sampling of inspections

**Inspection Failure**: Failed inspection triggers auto-block until re-inspection passed.

---

### 4.3 Auto-Block Policy

**System auto-blocks trucks with**:

#### Expired Documents
- RC (Registration Certificate) expired
- Fitness Certificate expired
- Insurance Certificate expired
- National Permit expired
- Pollution Certificate (PUC) expired

#### Failed Inspections
- Inspection failed quality check
- Compliance violations detected
- Safety issues identified

#### Missing Inspection
- Inspection overdue (>120 days)
- No inspection scheduled
- Inspection not completed

#### Eligibility Mismatch
- Truck does not meet eligibility criteria
- Pre-onboarding or post-onboarding mismatch
- Status change to ineligible

#### Unsafe Condition Reports
- Safety hazard reported
- Unsafe condition identified
- Roadworthiness concerns

**Auto-Block Process**:
1. System detects violation
2. Automatic block applied immediately
3. Operator notified
4. Compliance restoration required before unblocking

---

### 4.4 Alternate Truck Safety

**Alternate Truck Assignment**:

#### Allowed Only For:
- **Breakdown**: Mechanical failure requiring replacement
- **Accident**: Vehicle accident requiring replacement
- **Safety Hazard**: Unsafe condition requiring replacement

#### Every Alternate Truck is Treated as a Full Compliance Event
- Full eligibility verification required
- Complete compliance check
- Full inspection if needed
- Shipper approval mandatory
- Admin authorization required

**Alternate Truck Process**:
1. Breakdown/accident reported
2. Operator selects alternate truck
3. Full compliance verification
4. Shipper approves alternate driver
5. Admin authorizes alternate assignment
6. Alternate truck proceeds with shipment

**No Additional Fee**: Original bidding fee applies. No additional charges.

## üü© SECTION 5 ‚Äî BIDDING & COMMERCIAL CONDUCT POLICY

### 5.1 Fair Marketplace Rules

**Bidding Rules**:

#### One Active Bid per Operator
- Operator may place only one active bid per booking
- Submitting new bid cancels previous bid
- No multiple bids allowed

#### Unlimited Bid Modifications
- Operators may modify bids unlimited times
- No limit on number of modifications
- Subject to ledger balance availability

#### Ledger Cannot Go Negative
- Negative balance is prohibited
- Bid rejected if insufficient ledger balance
- Operator must maintain positive balance

#### Bidding Fee Auto-Deduction Only
- Bidding fee deducted automatically upon bid submission
- No manual fee modifications
- Fee calculation: ‚Çπ5 √ó tonnage + ‚Çπ0.25 √ó distance (km)

---

### 5.2 Price Transparency

**Price Visibility Rules**:

#### Shipper Sees Full Expected Price
- Shipper sees AI-estimated expected price
- Expected price not visible to operators
- Shipper uses expected price to set price range

#### Operator Sees Only Adjusted Price Range
- Operator sees only shipper-adjusted price range
- Cannot see expected price
- Cannot see other operators' bid amounts
- Fair bidding environment maintained

---

### 5.3 Auto-Finalization

**Auto-Finalization Policy**:

**Lowest bid wins automatically if shipper inactive for 24 hours**.

**Process**:
1. At least one bid exists on booking
2. Shipper inactive for exactly 24 hours
3. Lowest bid amount (ascending order) auto-accepted
4. Shipment created automatically
5. Shipper notified of auto-finalization

**No Refund**: Shipper cannot claim refund if auto-finalization occurred.

---

#### Fake Bids

- Bid manipulation

## üü¶ SECTION 6 ‚Äî SHIPMENT EXECUTION POLICY

### 6.1 Shipment Lifecycle

**Complete Shipment Lifecycle Sequence**:

1. **Booking**: Shipper creates load request
2. **Bidding**: Operators place bids
3. **Acceptance**: Shipper accepts bid or auto-finalization occurs
4. **Assignment**: Operator assigns driver and truck
5. **Approval**: Shipper approves driver
6. **Transit**: Driver picks up cargo and transports
7. **OTP**: Driver enters OTP for delivery completion
8. **POD**: Driver uploads Proof of Delivery PDF
9. **Completion**: Shipment marked complete

**No Skipping**: Statuses must follow sequence. Cannot skip or bypass any step.

---

### 6.2 Driver Assignment Rules

**Driver Assignment Requirements**:

#### Must Be Linked to Operator
- Driver must be linked to operator account
- Operator manages driver relationships
- Driver cannot self-assign

#### Must Be Approved by Shipper
- Shipper approval mandatory
- Cannot bypass approval step
- Approval required before shipment proceeds

#### Must Be KYC Verified
- Driver KYC must be approved
- Unverified drivers cannot be assigned
- KYC verification status checked

#### Cannot Have Multiple Active Shipments
- One active shipment per driver only
- Cannot assign driver with active shipment
- Driver must complete current shipment first

---

### 6.3 Tracking Policy

**GPS Tracking Requirements**:

#### GPS Ping Every 60 Seconds
- Driver app sends GPS location every 60 seconds
- Continuous tracking during active shipment
- Automated transmission

#### Alert if No Ping for 30 Minutes
- System alerts if no GPS ping for 30+ minutes
- Alert sent to operator, shipper, and HQ
- Investigation triggered

#### Real-Time Monitoring
- Real-time GPS tracking available
- Shipper can monitor shipment location
- Operator can monitor driver location
- HQ can monitor for compliance

**Tracking Violations**: Missing tracking triggers compliance escalation and potential block.

---

### 6.4 Delivery Policy

**Delivery Completion Requirements**:

#### Pickup Photo
- Driver must upload pickup photo
- Photo must show cargo being loaded
- Geotagged and timestamped

#### Drop Photo
- Driver must upload drop photo
- Photo must show cargo being unloaded
- Geotagged and timestamped

#### OTP Mandatory
- 6-digit OTP generated by system
- 24-hour validity period
- Driver must enter OTP to complete delivery
- No manual override without HQ authorization

#### POD PDF Mandatory
- Proof of Delivery PDF must be uploaded
- Contains delivery confirmation
- Typically includes recipient signature or acknowledgment
- Required for audit and legal purposes

**All Requirements Must Be Met**: Delivery cannot complete without all requirements satisfied.

#### Driver Mismatch
- Driver executing shipment not the approved driver

#### OTP Mismatch

## üü´ SECTION 7 ‚Äî PAYMENT & FINANCE POLICY

### 7.1 Cash-Only Payments

**Policy**: All shipper-to-operator/driver payments are cash only.

**Payment Method**:
- **Cash only** at delivery location
- No online payments
- No UPI transfers
- No bank transfers
- No digital settlements
- No credit card payments

**Enforcement**:
- Platform designed for cash transactions only
- No payment gateway integration for shipper payments
- Payment occurs at delivery location
- OTP completion occurs with payment

**Violation**: Attempting to process non-cash payments triggers investigation and potential block.

---

### 7.2 Bidding Fee Policy

**Bidding Fee Calculation**:

**Formula**: **Bidding Fee = ‚Çπ5 √ó tonnage + ‚Çπ0.25 √ó distance (km)**

**Example**:
- Tonnage: 10 tons
- Distance: 500 km
- Bidding Fee = (‚Çπ5 √ó 10) + (‚Çπ0.25 √ó 500) = ‚Çπ50 + ‚Çπ125 = ‚Çπ175

**Fee Deduction**:
- Automatically deducted from operator ledger upon bid submission
- Bid rejected if ledger balance insufficient
- Fee charged only once per active bid (modifications don't incur additional fees)

**No Exceptions**: Fee structure is fixed and cannot be modified.

---

### 7.3 Refund Policy

**No Refunds Policy**:

**No refunds except**:

#### Payment Failure
- Bidding fee deducted but bid not created (technical error)
- Transaction failure during deduction
- System error preventing bid creation

#### Technical Error
- Multiple deductions for single bid (system bug)
- Incorrect gateway deduction
- System malfunction

**No Refund Scenarios**:
- Bid accepted by shipper
- Bid rejected by shipper
- Bid withdrawn by operator
- Booking cancelled after bids placed
- Shipment completed

**Refund Process**: Approved refunds processed within 7-14 business days after verification.

---

### 7.4 Ledger Policy

**Ledger Management Rules**:

#### No Negative Balance
- Ledger cannot go below zero
- Hard limit enforced by system
- Bid rejected if balance insufficient

#### Operator Responsible for Maintaining Positive Ledger
- Operator must add funds to ledger
- Operator manages own ledger balance
- No credit extensions or loans

#### Ledger Operations
- Operator can add funds to ledger
- Operator can view balance and transaction history
- Bidding fees automatically deducted
- No manual ledger modifications allowed

**Immutable Ledger**: Ledger balances cannot be manually modified by any role (system-only deductions).

---

### 7.5 Franchise Revenue Policy

**Franchise Payment Structure**:

#### Unit + District Franchises Do Not Collect Commissions
- No commission fees on shipments
- No percentage-based fees
- No transaction-based payments

#### All Payouts Controlled by HQ
- HQ manages franchise revenue share
- Performance-based payout structure
- Franchise agreements define payout terms

#### Revenue Share Model
- Franchises earn revenue share from assigned operational roles
- Based on franchise performance and compliance
- Paid by HQ according to franchise agreement
- Not tied to individual bookings or shipments

**Zero-Commission Marketplace**: Operators pay only bidding fees. No commission on shipments.
