This Command Chain Architecture establishes:

- **Who holds authority**
- **Who approves what**
- **Who escalates to whom**

## üèõÔ∏è SECTION 1 ‚Äî THE CORE COMMAND PYRAMID

Rodistaa's authority structure is **hierarchical and non-negotiable**:

```
                Managing Director (Rodistaa)
                           ‚îÇ
                           ‚ñº
                     HQ Admin / Compliance
                           ‚îÇ
                           ‚ñº
                   District Franchise Head
                           ‚îÇ
                           ‚ñº
                    Unit Franchise Manager
                           ‚îÇ
                           ‚ñº
             Operators ‚Üí Drivers ‚Üí Shipments
                           ‚îÇ
                           ‚ñº
                     Shippers (Demand)
```

**This pyramid defines ultimate decision-making authority.**

### Authority Flow

**Top-Down Authority**:
- Managing Director ‚Üí HQ Admin ‚Üí District Franchise ‚Üí Unit Franchise ‚Üí Operators/Drivers
- Each level supervises and commands the level below
- Authority flows downward, escalations flow upward

**Decision Hierarchy**:
- Lower levels cannot override higher-level decisions
- Higher levels can override lower-level decisions (with proper authorization)
- System auto-blocks cannot be overridden without HQ authorization

**Supervision Chain**:
- Unit Franchise supervises local operations
- District Franchise supervises Unit Franchises
- HQ Admin supervises District Franchises
- Managing Director supervises HQ Admin

## üü• SECTION 2 ‚Äî HIGHEST AUTHORITY (Managing Director)

The Managing Director has **absolute authority** over all Rodistaa operations.

### Managing Director Authority

#### Absolute Authority Over Business Rules
- **Modify zero-compromise rules** (Section 2 of Lawbook)
- **Change business model** parameters
- **Update compliance policies**
- **Modify escalation procedures**
- **Define new business rules**

#### Final Veto on Escalations
- **Final decision** on all escalated cases
- **Override HQ Admin** decisions
- **Approve or reject** appeals
- **Make binding judgments** on disputes

#### Top Approval for Compliance Exceptions
- **Authorize compliance exceptions** (when justified)
- **Override auto-blocks** with proper justification
- **Approve special cases** requiring flexibility
- **Grant exceptions** to standard procedures

#### Power to Alter the Business Model
- **Modify platform rules**
- **Change operational procedures**
- **Update franchise agreements**
- **Revise compliance requirements**

#### Permission to Override Any Decision at Any Level
- **Override shipments**
- **Override driver assignments**
- **Override truck assignments**
- **Override franchise decisions**
- **Override admin decisions**
- **Override system auto-blocks** (with justification)

### Managing Director Cannot

**Cannot be overridden by any other role** (absolute authority)

**No limitations** - Managing Director is the ultimate authority

### Decision Process

1. **Escalation Received**: Case escalated from HQ Admin
2. **Review**: MD reviews all evidence and documentation
3. **Decision**: MD makes final binding decision
4. **Implementation**: Decision implemented immediately
5. **No Appeal**: MD decision is final (no further escalation)

## üüß SECTION 3 ‚Äî HQ ADMIN (Central Command)

HQ Admin functions as the **operational command center** for all platform operations.

### HQ Admin Authority

#### Override Authority
- **Override shipments**: Change shipment status, lifecycle, or assignments
- **Override drivers**: Reassign drivers (with shipper approval)
- **Override trucks**: Reassign trucks (with shipper approval)
- **Override routes**: Modify shipment routes if required

#### User Management
- **Block/unblock users**: Operators, drivers, shippers (except MD override cases)
- **Suspend accounts**: Temporary or permanent suspensions
- **Manage user access**: Grant or revoke platform access

#### Operational Decisions
- **Approve alternate truck requests**: Authorize alternate truck assignments for breakdowns/accidents
- **Resolve disputes**: Handle shipper-operator disputes with binding decisions
- **Initiate fraud investigations**: Launch investigations on suspicious activity
- **Handle escalations**: Process escalations from District Franchise

#### Franchise Management
- **Approve franchise payouts**: Authorize revenue share payments to franchises
- **Manage franchise performance**: Track and evaluate franchise operations
- **Enforce franchise compliance**: Apply strike system and penalties

#### Compliance Oversight
- **Override auto-blocks**: With proper justification and authorization
- **Handle compliance violations**: Process Category D+ violations
- **Maintain audit trails**: Ensure all actions are logged

### HQ Admin Cannot

#### Financial Restrictions
- **Cannot modify ledger**: Ledger is immutable (system-only modifications)
- **Cannot access financial data**: Limited to viewing only (no modifications)

#### KYC Restrictions
- **Cannot access full KYC** unless has KYC-admin role
- **Cannot view unmasked data** without authorization
- **Cannot modify KYC** (only KYC-admin can approve/reject)

#### Administrative Restrictions
- **Cannot create new franchises** without MD approval
- **Cannot modify business rules** (MD authority only)
- **Cannot override document expiry rules** without justification
- **Cannot lower compliance flags** without evidence

#### System Restrictions
- **Cannot override system auto-blocks** without proper justification
- **Cannot bypass zero-compromise rules** (Section 2 of Lawbook)

### HQ Admin Position

**HQ Admin sits at the top operational tier, just below the MD.**

**Authority Level**: Highest operational authority (subject only to MD)

**Supervision**: Supervised by Managing Director

**Supervision Of**: District Franchises and all operational activities

## üü® SECTION 4 ‚Äî COMPLIANCE/KYC-ADMIN (Special Role)

The KYC-Admin is a **trusted, restricted HQ role** specializing in identity verification and fraud detection.

### KYC-Admin Authority

#### Identity Verification
- **Approve or reject KYC**: Final authority on KYC submissions
- **Detect identity fraud**: Identify fraudulent or suspicious KYC
- **Handle suspicious KYC submissions**: Investigate and process flagged KYC
- **Provide identity confirmation**: Confirm identities during investigations

#### Data Access
- **Access full KYC documents**: Only role allowed to view complete, unmasked KYC
- **Access unmasked personal information**: Full visibility for verification purposes
- **Access audit logs**: View KYC access logs and verification history

#### Fraud Detection
- **Flag fraudulent identities**: Mark suspicious KYC for investigation
- **Report identity theft**: Escalate confirmed identity theft cases
- **Coordinate with legal**: Support legal escalation for fraud cases

### KYC-Admin Cannot (No Authority Over)

#### Operational Restrictions
- **No authority over shipments**: Cannot modify, override, or assign shipments
- **No authority over bidding**: Cannot interfere with bidding process
- **No authority over ledger**: Cannot access or modify financial data
- **No authority over franchise payouts**: Cannot manage franchise payments
- **No authority over truck assignments**: Cannot assign or reassign trucks
- **No authority over price negotiations**: Cannot interfere with pricing

#### Administrative Restrictions
- **Cannot approve drivers**: Driver approval is shipper/admin authority
- **Cannot approve trucks**: Truck approval is franchise authority
- **Cannot override shipments**: Shipment override is HQ Admin authority
- **Cannot modify bookings**: Booking modification is shipper authority

### KYC-Admin Position

**KYC Admin = Identity Expert, not operational authority.**

**Authority Level**: Specialized authority for KYC and identity verification only

**Supervision**: Supervised by HQ Admin

**Supervision Of**: KYC verification processes only

**Access Level**: Highest access to identity data, no operational authority

## üü© SECTION 5 ‚Äî DISTRICT FRANCHISE COMMAND LEVEL

District Franchise Head **commands all units in the district** and serves as regional governance.

### District Authority

#### Inspection Oversight
- **Audit all Unit Franchise inspections**: Review inspection quality and accuracy
- **Reject or approve inspections**: Can override Unit Franchise inspection decisions
- **Set inspection quality standards**: Define quality requirements for units

#### Franchise Management
- **Issue strikes to Unit Franchises**: Apply penalty system for violations
- **Set regional operational targets**: Define performance goals for units
- **Monitor compliance performance**: Track unit compliance metrics

#### Escalation Management
- **Escalate high-risk cases to HQ**: Forward Category C+ violations to HQ
- **Monitor accident/breakdown escalations**: Oversee emergency response
- **Enforce compliance timeline**: Ensure timely compliance resolution

#### Quality Control
- **Review inspection reports**: Audit Unit Franchise inspection documentation
- **Conduct quality audits**: Random sampling of unit inspections
- **Train Unit Franchises**: Provide training on compliance requirements

### District Cannot

#### Access Restrictions
- **Cannot access full KYC**: Masked details only
- **Cannot view unmasked data**: Privacy protection enforced

#### Administrative Restrictions
- **Cannot modify payouts**: Franchise payout management is HQ authority
- **Cannot create new franchises**: Franchise creation is HQ/MD authority
- **Cannot override HQ/Admin decisions**: Must follow HQ directives

#### Operational Restrictions
- **Cannot modify shipments**: Shipment modification is operator/admin authority
- **Cannot modify bookings**: Booking modification is shipper authority
- **Cannot override auto-blocks**: Auto-block override is HQ authority

### District Franchise Position

**District Franchise is the regional governance layer.**

**Authority Level**: Regional supervisory authority

**Supervision**: Supervised by HQ Admin

**Supervision Of**: All Unit Franchises in the district

**Geographic Scope**: District-level operations

---

## üü¶ SECTION 6 ‚Äî UNIT FRANCHISE FIELD COMMAND

Unit Franchise **manages local operations** and serves as field-level compliance enforcement.

### Unit Authority

#### Inspection Authority
- **Conduct physical truck inspections**: Mandatory 120-day inspection cycle
- **Approve or reject truck eligibility**: Final decision on truck compliance
- **Upload inspection photos**: With geotag and timestamp
- **Complete inspection reports**: Document inspection findings

#### Compliance Enforcement
- **Submit compliance reports**: Report violations and issues
- **Enforce operator education**: Train operators on compliance
- **Support operators in onboarding**: Assist with truck registration

#### Escalation Authority
- **Raise escalations to District**: Forward issues beyond Unit authority
- **Flag potential fraud**: Report suspicious activities immediately

### Unit Cannot

#### Access Restrictions
- **Cannot access full KYC**: Masked details only
- **Cannot view unmasked data**: Privacy protection enforced

#### Operational Restrictions
- **Cannot modify shipments**: Shipment modification is operator/admin authority
- **Cannot assign drivers**: Driver assignment is operator authority
- **Cannot approve alternate truck**: Alternate truck approval is admin authority

#### Administrative Restrictions
- **Cannot modify financial data**: No financial access
- **Cannot override District/HQ decisions**: Must follow District directives
- **Cannot override auto-blocks**: Auto-block override is HQ authority

### Unit Franchise Position

**Unit Franchise = field execution + compliance enforcement.**

**Authority Level**: Local/field-level authority

**Supervision**: Supervised by District Franchise

**Supervision Of**: Local operators and trucks

**Geographic Scope**: Local/unit-level operations

---

## üü™ SECTION 7 ‚Äî OPERATOR COMMAND SCOPE

Operator is the **fleet manager + commercial participant** with control over their trucks and drivers.

### Operator Authority

#### Truck Management
- **Onboard trucks** (max 10): Register and manage fleet
- **Link drivers**: Associate drivers with operator account
- **Assign drivers & trucks**: Assign to shipments (subject to shipper approval)

#### Bidding Authority
- **Place bids**: Submit bids on bookings (one active bid per booking)
- **Modify bids**: Unlimited modifications (subject to ledger availability)

#### Financial Management
- **Manage ledger**: Add funds, track balance, view transaction history
- **View bidding fees**: See fee calculations and deductions

#### Operational Authority
- **Request alternate trucks**: Request alternate truck for breakdown/accident
- **Handle breakdowns**: Report and coordinate breakdown response
- **Track shipments**: View tracking for assigned shipments

### Operator Cannot

#### Approval Restrictions
- **Cannot approve drivers**: Driver approval is shipper authority (operator assigns, shipper approves)
- **Cannot modify booking**: Booking modification is shipper authority
- **Cannot cancel shipments**: Shipment cancellation is shipper/admin authority

#### Access Restrictions
- **Cannot view shipper full contact**: Masked phone numbers only
- **Cannot view other operators' bids**: Bid amounts are private

#### Compliance Restrictions
- **Cannot override system auto-blocks**: Auto-block override is HQ authority
- **Cannot override document expiry**: Document expiry enforcement is automatic
- **Cannot bypass compliance**: Must meet all eligibility requirements

### Operator Position

**Operator = fleet manager + commercial participant.**

**Authority Level**: Commercial participant authority

**Supervision**: Supervised by Unit/District Franchise for compliance

**Supervision Of**: Own trucks and linked drivers

**Business Role**: Marketplace participant (supply side)

---

## üü´ SECTION 8 ‚Äî DRIVER COMMAND SCOPE

Driver is the **execution-level role** responsible for shipment execution.

### Driver Authority

#### Shipment Execution
- **Accept shipment assigned**: Accept shipment from operator
- **Execute pickup & drop**: Perform pickup and delivery operations
- **Upload photos**: Upload pickup and drop photos (mandatory)
- **Upload POD**: Upload Proof of Delivery PDF

#### Delivery Completion
- **Enter OTP**: Enter shipper-provided OTP to complete delivery
- **Complete shipment**: Finalize delivery upon correct OTP entry

#### Operational Reporting
- **Report delay/breakdown**: Report delays or mechanical failures
- **Update shipment status**: Update status through driver app

### Driver Cannot

#### Assignment Restrictions
- **Cannot assign self to a shipment**: Assignment is operator/admin authority
- **Cannot start a shipment without approval**: Requires shipper approval first

#### Modification Restrictions
- **Cannot modify shipment fields**: Shipment details are fixed
- **Cannot complete shipment without OTP**: OTP is mandatory for completion
- **Cannot switch to another operator mid-shipment**: Operator assignment is fixed

#### Access Restrictions
- **Cannot see shipper's contact** (masked): Privacy protection enforced
- **Cannot view KYC of others**: Identity data is restricted

#### Operational Restrictions
- **Cannot modify booking**: Booking modification is shipper authority
- **Cannot modify pricing**: Pricing is operator/shipper controlled
- **Cannot bypass compliance**: Must meet all compliance requirements

### Driver Position

**Driver = execution-level role.**

**Authority Level**: Execution-level authority

**Supervision**: Supervised by Operator (for assignments) and Franchise (for compliance)

**Supervision Of**: Own shipment execution only

**Business Role**: Service executor

---

## üüß SECTION 9 ‚Äî SHIPPER COMMAND SCOPE

Shipper is the **demand-side commander of bookings** with control over booking creation and acceptance.

### Shipper Authority

#### Booking Management
- **Create bookings**: Post load requests with origin, destination, cargo details
- **Modify booking** (before bids): Adjust booking details before bids are placed
- **Set price range**: Adjust price range from expected price

#### Bidding Authority
- **Negotiate bids**: Unlimited negotiations with operators
- **Accept bids**: Select winning bid to create shipment
- **View bids**: See all bids with masked operator details

#### Shipment Control
- **Approve drivers**: Mandatory approval of operator-assigned driver
- **Track shipment**: Real-time GPS tracking of active shipments
- **Complete shipment**: Provide OTP to driver for delivery completion
- **Receive POD**: Receive Proof of Delivery from driver

### Shipper Cannot

#### Assignment Restrictions
- **Cannot assign drivers**: Driver assignment is operator authority (shipper approves)
- **Cannot assign trucks**: Truck assignment is operator authority

#### Modification Restrictions
- **Cannot modify shipment route**: Route is fixed after shipment creation
- **Cannot modify booking after bids**: Booking locked after bids placed
- **Cannot override compliance flags**: Compliance is system-enforced

#### Access Restrictions
- **Cannot access operator/driver numbers** (masked): Privacy protection enforced
- **Cannot view full KYC**: KYC access is KYC-admin only
- **Cannot edit truck information**: Truck information is operator-controlled

### Shipper Position

**Shipper = demand-side commander of bookings.**

**Authority Level**: Demand-side authority

**Supervision**: Not supervised by franchises (independent participant)

**Supervision Of**: Own bookings and shipments

**Business Role**: Marketplace participant (demand side)

## üü® SECTION 10 ‚Äî SYSTEM COMMAND (Automatic Authority)

Rodistaa System has **mandatory auto-actions** that enforce business rules impartially.

### System Auto-Actions

#### Automated Decision-Making
- **Auto-finalize lowest bid**: After 24-hour shipper inactivity
- **Auto-block trucks with expired documents**: Immediate enforcement
- **Auto-block drivers with KYC mismatch**: Identity verification enforcement
- **Auto-block operators with fraudulent behavior**: Fraud detection enforcement

#### Automated Monitoring
- **Auto-alert tracking failure** (>30min): GPS ping monitoring
- **Auto-validate driver availability**: One active shipment per driver
- **Auto-route escalations**: Route escalations to appropriate authority

#### Automated Compliance
- **Auto-block expired documents**: Document expiry enforcement
- **Auto-block overdue inspections**: Inspection cycle enforcement
- **Auto-block negative ledger**: Financial compliance enforcement
- **Auto-block multiple bids**: Bidding rule enforcement

### Cannot Be Overridden (System Rules)

#### Financial Rules
- **Ledger cannot go negative**: Hard limit, no exceptions
- **Bidding fee auto-deduction**: Automatic, no manual modification

#### Compliance Rules
- **Truck eligibility rules**: HGV, 2018+, BS4/BS6, NP requirements
- **Document expiry blocks**: Automatic enforcement, no bypass
- **BS4/BS6 requirement**: Emission standard enforcement

#### Operational Rules
- **One active bid rule**: One bid per operator per booking
- **OTP cannot be bypassed**: OTP is mandatory for completion
- **One active shipment per driver**: Hard limit enforcement

### System Position

**System = the ultimate impartial enforcer.**

**Authority Level**: Automated enforcement authority

**Supervision**: Monitored by HQ Admin

**Supervision Of**: All automated compliance and rule enforcement

**Override Authority**: Only HQ Admin can override (with justification) or MD (absolute)

## üü• SECTION 11 ‚Äî ESCALATION PATHWAYS

Escalation pathways define **who escalates to whom** based on severity and authority.

### 1. Operational Issue (Low Severity)

**Path**: Driver/Operator ‚Üí Unit Franchise

**Examples**:
- Minor inspection delays
- Process clarifications
- Training needs
- Non-critical documentation issues

**Timeframe**: Resolved within 48 hours

**Authority**: Unit Franchise has full authority

---

### 2. Compliance Issue (Medium Severity)

**Path**: Unit Franchise ‚Üí District Franchise

**Examples**:
- Document expiry issues
- Inspection quality concerns
- Minor tracking anomalies
- Performance issues

**Timeframe**: Escalated within 24 hours, resolved within 5 business days

**Authority**: District Franchise has full authority

---

### 3. Fraud/Risk Issue (High Severity)

**Path**: District Franchise ‚Üí HQ Admin ‚Üí Compliance Team

**Examples**:
- Suspicious KYC submissions
- Fake document attempts
- Tracking manipulation attempts
- Collusion patterns

**Timeframe**: Escalated immediately, investigation within 24 hours

**Authority**: HQ Admin has full authority, Compliance Team supports

---

### 4. Critical Issue (Severe)

**Path**: HQ Admin ‚Üí Managing Director (Final Decision Authority)

**Examples**:
- Confirmed identity theft
- Document forgery confirmed
- Fraudulent POD confirmed
- Criminal activity detected
- System-level policy changes

**Timeframe**: Immediate escalation, MD decision within 7 business days

**Authority**: Managing Director has absolute authority

---

### 5. KYC Issue

**Path**: Driver/Operator ‚Üí KYC Admin ‚Üí HQ Compliance

**Examples**:
- KYC verification issues
- Identity mismatch
- Suspicious KYC submissions
- KYC fraud detection

**Timeframe**: KYC Admin review within 24 hours, escalation to HQ if needed

**Authority**: KYC Admin has KYC authority, HQ Compliance handles escalation

## üü© SECTION 12 ‚Äî OVERRIDE HIERARCHY

Only specific roles can override business flows, with clear hierarchy and restrictions.

### Override Level 1 ‚Äî Admin (HQ)

**HQ Admin can override**:

#### Shipment Override
- **Override shipment status**: Change shipment lifecycle status
- **Override shipment route**: Modify delivery route if required
- **Override shipment timeline**: Adjust pickup/delivery schedules

#### Assignment Override
- **Override driver assignment**: Reassign driver (with shipper approval)
- **Override truck assignment**: Reassign truck (with shipper approval)

#### Operational Override
- **Override system auto-blocks**: With proper justification and authorization
- **Override compliance flags**: With evidence and documentation
- **Override shipment completion**: In exceptional circumstances

**Requirements**:
- HQ authorization (self-authorization for HQ Admin)
- Justification documentation
- Audit trail maintenance
- Shipper approval for driver/truck changes

---

### Override Level 2 ‚Äî Managing Director

**Managing Director can override**:

#### System-Level Business Rules
- **Override zero-compromise rules** (Section 2 of Lawbook)
- **Override auto-block rules**: With absolute authority
- **Override compliance requirements**: With proper justification

#### Compliance Exceptions
- **Grant compliance exceptions**: Authorize special cases
- **Override document expiry blocks**: With justification
- **Override eligibility requirements**: In exceptional cases

#### Franchise Penalties
- **Override franchise strikes**: Reverse or modify penalties
- **Override franchise terminations**: Reverse termination decisions
- **Modify franchise agreements**: Update franchise terms

#### User Reinstatement
- **Override permanent blocks**: Reinstatement after permanent ban
- **Override suspensions**: Reverse suspension decisions
- **Override user terminations**: Restore terminated accounts

**Requirements**:
- MD absolute authority (no additional authorization needed)
- Justification documentation (for audit)
- Policy update if rule modification

---

### Override Restrictions

#### No Override Authority
- **Unit Franchise**: Cannot override any decisions
- **District Franchise**: Cannot override HQ/MD decisions
- **Operator**: Cannot override system or admin decisions
- **Driver**: Cannot override any decisions
- **Shipper**: Cannot override system or admin decisions
- **KYC-Admin**: Cannot override operational decisions

**Only HQ Admin and Managing Director have override authority.**

## üü¶ SECTION 13 ‚Äî COMMAND LIMITATIONS SUMMARY

Clear boundaries define **what each role cannot do**.

### Command Limitations Matrix

| Role | What They Cannot Do |
|------|---------------------|
| **Shipper** | View phone numbers (masked only), assign trucks/drivers, modify bookings after bids, override compliance flags, access operator/driver KYC |

| **Unit Franchise** | Access full KYC (masked only), change shipments, assign drivers, approve alternate truck, modify financial data, override District/HQ decisions |
| **District Franchise** | Create franchises (HQ authority), access full KYC (masked only), modify payouts (HQ authority), override HQ/Admin decisions, modify shipments/bookings |
| **Admin (HQ)** | Modify ledger (immutable), access full KYC without KYC-admin role, create franchises without MD, override document expiry rules without justification, lower compliance flags without evidence |
| **KYC-Admin** | Modify bookings/shipments, approve drivers, approve trucks, override shipments, access ledger, manage franchise payouts |

**These are hard boundaries.**
